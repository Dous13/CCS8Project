Introduction

Welcome to our laundry service website, created by a team of Computer Science Students of Silliman University for our CCS 8 Project. The Website helps you book, contact, and plan out laundry services for delivery or pick up. The website is an example of a laundry service that you can get online without any hassle, helping those who have no time to wash their clothes. The Site shows fundementals of creating a website for differently abled users, and ensures that the website is fully functional with different examples. Model used are mostly men to break the stigma that women always and must do the laundry. With our user-friendly online platform, scheduling a pickup and delivery is fast and easy. Let us take care of your laundry needs while you focus on what matters most.
