Who Are We?

The Laundry Science provides full-service for your laundry needs. We are committed to providing quality laundry and dry-cleaning services by using safe and gentle laundry detergents and dry-cleaning products. Our team is dedicated to handling your clothes with care. We pick up and drop off your clothes right at your place of work or your home.

Personalized Experience

You Can Always Reach Us For Laundry Concerns. We Are Happy To Help You With Your Laundry Needs. Conact Us And We Will Help You With What You Need.

Pricing

The Quality And Cost, Determine The Price. Trust Our Pricing Is Reasonable & Affordable. Quality Comes With Cost, And The Cost Will Be Absolutely Affordable.

Convenience

We simplify the booking request. Simply book through our website or SMS us for pick up request. Our friendly staff will contact you to confirm your request.

Quality

We take utmost care of your clothes, segregating the whites and colored clothes, as well as the dark colored ones to light. We use gentle yet effective cleaning detergents to ensure that it will not damage your clothes especially the delicates.

Express Delivery

We offer a rush laundry service if required. We can deliver within 8-24 hours. We ensure you that your clothes come back to you safe and sound without any hassle, without any damages, without any losses, and without any problems. Delivery with no problems.

Order Update

We immediately notify you once the laundry is done and ready for delivery. Let us know your convenient time to accept the laundry so we can schedule accordingly. Contact us through email or phone we will surely assist you with our utmost quality.
